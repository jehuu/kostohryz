RIDER NOTES

  • Set length: 15 min.
  • Soundcheck preferred.
  • Noise and glitch friendly front of house approach.

TECHNICAL

  • FOH should keep drums and triggers aggressive but readable.

MONITORS

  • 4 monitor wedges (3 downstage + 1 upstage left of drums).

BACKLINE

  • Guitar pedalboard switcher feeds two amps.
  • Both amps are main; one is noise-focused.
  • Please mic both cabs if possible.

DRUMS + TRIGGERS

  • 2x22 Kick drum, 16 floor tom, 14 rack tom, 2x14 snare.
  • Snare and kicks are captured by triggers.
  • Trigger signal is routed to Mixer 2 and blended into final mix.
