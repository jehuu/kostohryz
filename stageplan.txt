GENERAL DESCRIPTION
- KOSTOHRYZ plays experimental grindcore: very loud, fast, and physically aggressive.
- The set blends tight rhythmic violence with unstable textures, feedback, and harsh noise.
- Dynamic range is intentional: abrupt jumps between dense walls of sound and exposed moments.

INSTRUMENT GROUP NOTES
- DRUMS: Acoustic core of the set, tuned for impact and articulation under extreme volume.
- DRUM TRIGGERS: Add precision attack and electronic reinforcement for kicks and snare accents.
- GUITAR: Main harmonic/noise engine, shifting between grind riffs, drones, and abrasive textures.
- GUITAR PEDALBOARD: Primary tone-shaping hub; distortion, gating, and modulation create layered chaos.
- AMP 1 + CAB 1: Mid-forward articulation and cut for riff clarity.
- AMP 2 + CAB 2: Extended low-end mass and sustain to widen the guitar wall.
- KEYS: Harmonic beds, dissonant clusters, and synthetic tension between heavy sections.
- NOISE RIG: Generates glitches, bursts, static, and non-musical gestures as a structural element.
- SAXOPHONE: Raw acoustic voice for shrieks, overblown textures, and free-form counterlines.
- MIXER 1: Submix point for keys/noise sources before transfer to the central mix path.
- MIXER 2: Central merge point for drums, triggers, and electronic layers on stage.
- VOCAL MIC: Frontline focal point for extreme vocal delivery and command cues.
- MONITORS: Three monitor positions support timing lock and high-noise performance awareness.
